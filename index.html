[001] Who am I?

I am a Dutch computer science graduate from TU Delft (Msc, summer 2024). At the moment I am doing some side projects and in the meantime I am actively looking for a IT-related job in The Netherlands.

________________________________________________________________________________


[002] Why this website?

I strongly echo the words of Arya Kaul. Back in the days, before I was born, there used to be a time where the web was this geeky place connecting people all over the world, not through the big social media platforms like Facebook or Instagram, but through your own HTML/CSS created webpage. I really sparks one's creativity. Hence, to join the community, I created this personal website. Want to know what's possible with bare CSS, for example? Checkout https://csszengarden.com/ for cool ideas. This webpage's design was inspired by https://kisslinux.github.io/.
