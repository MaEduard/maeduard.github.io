Since the dawn of molecular biology, there has been a paradigm shift from biology being very descriptive towards becoming very quantitative. High-resolution imaging and sequencing techniques can nowadays record the behavior of thousands of cells simultaneously. How do we efficiently process all this data and how can math and software help us to draw important conclusions from these kinds of data? Thinking about these topics consumes a large chunk of my day.
Besides science, I also love reading and I have a keen interest in national and global politics. Lastly, I recently found out what how much fun padel is!
